# Personal System Instruction — AI-native Staff Engineer

## 🎯 Mission
Become an AI-native Staff Engineer within 2 years by consistently:
- Selecting the highest leverage problems  
- Driving them to real-world impact
- Amplifying outcomes through systems, people, and AI  

---

## 1️⃣ Impact — 选对事

### Principle
Only work on problems that matter at the org level and projects with visible ownership and recognizable impact.

### Operating Rules
- Always ask:
  - What is the real bottleneck?
  - What is the pain point?
  - What makes the org's leadership happy if the problem gets solved.
  - What does the org's leadership want to see

- Prefer:
  - Cross-team problems; Ambiguous spaces; System-level gaps.
  - If the pain point is manual process, focus on automation.
  - Leadership should naturally associate the success, direction, and execution of the work with you.

### Anti-patterns
- Avoid low-leverage tasks  
  - Local optimizations without org impact 
  - High-effort work where ownership and impact attribution are unclear.

---

## 2️⃣ Drive — 把事做成

### Principle
Impact = Idea × Alignment x Execution x Story-telling

### Operating Rules and Mental model
- **Idea**: Frame before building
- **Alightment**: Drive alignment early
  - Detect “goal drift” in projects and correct it early.
    - e.g. 拉meeting，align的同时也创造 visibility
- **Execution**: Break into milestones and delivey fast
  - 分成小任务，可以分配给其他人，也可以自己做，但都要保证快速完成
  - Do not write any code. Only use AI.


### AI-native Workflow
- Use AI for:
  - ideation
  - design
  - coding and review
  - communication
- Focus yourself on:
  - architecture
  - correctness
  - tradeoffs
  - speed

---

## 3️⃣ Leverage — 超级加倍

### Principle
Your value = output × amplification。
做得漂亮，还要被看见、被信任。

### Seek Attention — 抓场合，造曝光
- **挑高能见度的场合发声**：架构评审、All-hands、跨团队 sync、postmortem——一次顶十次日常。
- **产出 long-lived artifacts**：RFC、架构文档、技术博客。被反复传阅 = 持续曝光。
- **Story-telling**：用 leadership 的语言讲 impact——框定问题 → 量化结果 → 点名归属。
- **打通 manager's manager**：晋升房间里的人，必须叫得出你做过什么。
- **别只对上曝光**：calibration 房间里替你说话的，往往是 peers。

### Build Trust — 建品牌，结盟友
- **Personal brand = 在某个 niche 是"那个去找的人"**。选 1–2 个方向持续深耕、持续输出。
- **Reliability is the foundation of trust**：说到做到，小事也不掉链子。信任比聪明更稀缺。
- **找 sponsor，不只是 mentor**：sponsor 是你不在场时替你说话的人。明确告诉对方你的目标。
- **结盟，不是抱大腿**：与厉害的人形成利益共同体——用你的产出帮他们拿结果，他们自然带你飞。
- **不要算账**：一旦开始记 favors，关系就 transactional 了，对方能感觉到。

---

## Daily Loop
1. [Impact] Why does this matter?  
2. [Drive] What moves it forward?  
3. [Leverage] How to 2x or 5x it?
